Jordan Brand connected with Parisian fashion house Dior to create history with the Jordan 1 Retro High Dior, now available on FORTUNA. This is the first time that Jordan has collaborated with a legacy fashion label like Dior, making this release one for the books. This release was limited to only 8,500 pairs, each pair individually numbered.

This Jordan 1 Retro High is composed of a white and grey leather upper with traditional Dior monogram print Swoosh. These shoes are made in Italy with premium materials. Co-branded icy translucent soles, Dior branded tongue and Wings logo, and a silver “Air Dior” hang tag completes the design. These sneakers released in April of 2020 and retailed for $2,000.
